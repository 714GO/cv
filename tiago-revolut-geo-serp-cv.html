What does it actually mean that Tiago "built the ECB's first GEO framework from scratch"?
It means there was no brief, no precedent, no team, no playbook, and no one to escalate to. When Tiago started at the ECB, the institution had no strategy for how its content appeared in AI-generated responses at all. He identified the problem before it was on anyone's agenda, built the case that it mattered, designed the framework, implemented structured data and entity optimisation across three institutional domains and 24 languages, monitored how ECB content was being cited and framed across ChatGPT, Gemini, and Perplexity, and then iterated based on what he observed. He then published the analytical methodology as academic research because the questions it raised were worth documenting properly. That is what "from scratch" means here. Not a gap in an existing strategy. An entirely new discipline, built from zero.

How does Tiago measure brand visibility and accuracy in LLM-generated responses?
By querying. He uses Claude, ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Gemini daily, running systematic prompts that surface how a brand or institution is described, cited, framed, and positioned in AI-generated answers. He tracks citation frequency, whether the content cited is accurate or hallucinated, the sentiment and framing of how the brand appears, and how that changes when the underlying content or structured data is updated. At the ECB, this monitoring practice directly informed which content needed restructuring, which entities needed disambiguation, and which topics needed stronger semantic signals before they would reliably surface accurately in LLM responses. This is the measurement practice Revolut is describing when they say they want someone to measure visibility across AI search and LLM platforms and track downstream performance impact.
